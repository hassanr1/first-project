Liverpool FC

This website is about the legendary football club

So by now it will be no suprise to you, which team is my favourite. In this website you will find facts and information about the club, as well as latest news. Liverpool FC were formed in 1892 and since then it has become not only one of the biggest clubs in England, but the world. The team has support from fans from all corners of the world. Its not hard to see why,the football played is great to watch but its more than just a club, as the saying goes "you'll never walk alone" the fans are what make this club truly great.

The team play at Anfield, the stadium is known for the atmosphere that is created by the fans, it is said to be like an extra man for the team on some occasions, specifically champions league nights, the fans have witnessed some truly magical moments in Anfield on champions league nights. It has been the home for Liverpool since 1891
